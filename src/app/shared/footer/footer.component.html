About

We are the team known as Exilee Coders. We love and code Angular 2. We watch Star Wars movies. And laugh at Shai Reznik’s hilarious videos. So for this hackathon, we thought of building a mini app that might be useful to the wider community. The app shows the current status of Angular 2 related libraries/modules and a bit of Star Wars menace.
